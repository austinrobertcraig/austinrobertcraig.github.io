In 2002, the Port of Seattle and Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) reached a memorandum of understanding (MOA) with the Highline School District which outlined funding for noise mitigation in 15 Highline schools. The Port and FAA each committed $50 million to this effort. To date, the Port claims 10 schools have been completed. The objective of this project is to identify the causal effect of noise insulation projects on student outcomes in schools impacted by aviation noise pollution.

Click [here](/files/highline-pre-analysis-plan-final.pdf) to view the pre-analysis plan for this project.

The project has also been formally registered with the [Open Science Foundation](https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/K4XYR).
